What is Zenity?

🎮 Gamified Wellness

Zenity is a gamified mental health and habit-tracking web application designed to help users build better wellness routines while having fun. By blending psychology, habit-building, and gamification, we transform self-care into a rewarding and engaging daily practice.

🌟 More Than an App

We're not just another tracking app - we're your companion on the journey to better mental health. With XP systems, streaks, and community challenges, we make wellness feel like an adventure, not a chore.

Why We Built Zenity

😊 Friendly, not clinical

Mental health support should feel warm and approachable, not intimidating or sterile.

🎯 Engaging, not boring

Building healthy habits should be fun and motivating, not a tedious daily task.

🤝 Supportive, not lonely

Your wellness journey is better with a community of like-minded people cheering you on.

Zenity is our answer — a space where you can track your emotions, build habits, earn XP, and grow with a positive community.
